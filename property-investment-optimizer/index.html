Depreciation Deductions
Depreciation is a non-cash tax deduction that reduces your taxable rental income. Div 43 (building): 2.5%/yr of construction cost for 40 yrs (post-Sep 1987 builds). Div 40 (plant & equipment): fixtures, appliances etc — get a quantity surveyor report for accurate figures.
Note: Div 43 deductions do NOT reduce your CGT cost base. Div 40 cost base for each item IS reduced by claimed depreciation. This model uses a simplified cost base for CGT (purchase price + buying costs + capital improvements).

CGT Notes
Australia's 50% CGT discount applies when you hold the property for more than 12 months. Capital gain = Sale price − selling costs − cost base. The taxable gain (after discount) is added to your assessable income and taxed at your marginal rate.
Optimal sell timing: The Hold Period chart shows IRR and total profit for every possible sale year. IRR peaks earlier than total profit — consider your target metric.
